Career Opportunities

As an award-winning clean-tech startup, Watt-Learn is developing revolutionary AI-based software for the next generation of energy storage battery projects that will optimize the growth of renewable energy and reduce mankind’s reliance on fossil fuels.

We are looking for champions who are excited to make meaningful impacts on our mission by being part of Watt-Learn’s story in solving some of the world’s most pressing environmental challenges.

Watt-Learn is a startup founded by energy and software enthusiasts from Carnegie Mellon University and is now backed by one of the largest private energy investors in the country. That means Watt-Learn employees get the best of both worlds: security and benefits of a well-funded and stable company, and the excitement, visibility and growth opportunities of being part of a fast-growing and innovative startup.
