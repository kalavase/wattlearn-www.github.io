  • Battery Performance Audit

    existing projects

    If you wonder what our PLAAS solution can do for your project, and what is the size of unrealized opportunity left on the table, this is a service you would benefit from. We will work closely with you to customize our PLAAS solution to your project’s specifications. Going back in time, we will apply PLAAS on your project and demonstrate, in detail, the value that could have been generated for your project in variety of revenue streams (e.g., revenue/ saving, lifetime, cycles, etc.).

  • Real-time Recommendations

    existing projects

    If you feel that PLAAS is a right solution for your project (either because the results from the Battery Performance Audit is suggesting so, or because you believe in science and think that there must be a more intelligent way of operating batteries than the current state of the industry), BUT still prefer to have the control over operation of your battery project, this solution is for you. You will receive a forward-looking dispatch setpoints, on realtime basis, which act as recommendations that can be either incorporated into the battery control system, or used to shadow the existing strategies.

  • Proactive Lifetime-Aware Automation System (PLAAS)

    existing and future projects

    If you want to maximize your battery project, this solution is for you. PLAAS serves as an intelligence layer on top of the existing battery control system. PLAAS sends proactive setpoints to your battery’s management system to maximize a customized set of objectives for your very specific project (e.g., revenue/ savings, number of cycles, lifetime, carbon footprint, etc. or a combination of all). Our solution is non-intrusive, technology agnostic, and cloud-based.
