About

  • Humble beginnings

    2017

    Watt-Learn is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) startup raised from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). The founders; Julian Lamy and Matineh Eybpoosh, have decades of combined experience in applying AI and optimization for optimal operation of energy systems, power wholesale market, development and operation of energy storage and renewable projects, and data-driven diagnosis of energy systems.

  • Partnership

    November 2018

    On November 2018, Watt-Learn joined forces with one of the leader energy equity investment firms in the country. Watt-Learn will be operating a large pipeline of front-of-the-meter projects across the US to maximize the ROI for the shareholders.
